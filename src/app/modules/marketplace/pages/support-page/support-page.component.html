Frequently Asked Questions

General

How do I register on POP961?

If you want to register to buy products on POP961, all you need to do is click the “My Account” link on the top right corner of the page and then click on “Register” on the left side of the page and enter your information.

You can now start shopping on POP961!

What can I find on POP961?

From apparel, furniture and decor, to books, electronics and gadgets, POP961 gives you access to endless categories of top quality items.

Who supplies the products?

Countless items from the entire Lebanese market unite under one umbrella, that being POP961. On POP961 you will surely enjoy shopping for amazing products supplied by large top brands as well as unique articles sold by more traditional shops.

Can I review an item?

Of course! We would be happy to hear from you once you receive your item. Your opinion matters and sharing it will help us keep you satisfied!

To leave a review, go to the product’s page, scroll down to the “Reviews” section. Rate the product, and tell us what you honestly think about it in the “Your Review” text box. Do not forget to share good pictures of your item!

How do I filter the store products?

Once you click on a category item, you will see filters on the left side of the page. Tick the filters you want, and happy shopping! 🙂

What is your exchange policy?

In case of a defective product you can submit a dispute through our website. Go to My Account > Orders and click on the order you wish to dispute. You will be able to view the order details with a button labeled 'Open Dispute', click the button and fill the form. You will be able to view the progress of your dispute under My Account > Disputes.

Technical

I forgot my password. What do I do?

On the top right corner of the page, click on “My Account.” Then click on “Lost your password?” link. Enter your username or email then click on “Reset password.” An email with a link to reset your password will be sent to you. Click on the link provided in the email and then enter your new password.

I can't login. What do I do?

Hmm… It seems like you’ve forgotten your username or password. Try clicking on the “Lost your password?” link. In case you have forgotten your username, then please contact us for help.

How do I edit my account settings?

To edit you account settings, click on “My Account” in the top right corner of the page. To edit the shipping and/or billing addresses click on “Addresses”. To edit your Email address, or password, click on “Account details”.
